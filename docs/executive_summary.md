# Executive Summary

Employee attrition is a major challenge for HR departments because losing talent creates recruitment costs, operational disruption, and knowledge loss.

In this project, we design a trusted AI solution that helps HR teams identify employees at risk of leaving the company and understand the main drivers behind this risk.

Our approach combines predictive analytics with two responsible AI dimensions:
- **Ethical AI**, through fairness auditing across sensitive employee groups
- **Explainable AI**, through transparent explanations of model predictions

The objective is not to automate HR decisions, but to provide a reliable and interpretable decision-support system that helps HR teams act earlier and more fairly.
